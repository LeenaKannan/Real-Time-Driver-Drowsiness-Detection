1. Recommended Datasets
Primary Dataset: MRL Eye Dataset

Download: http://mrl.cs.vsb.cz/eyedataset
Size: 84,898 samples
Quality: High-resolution eye images (384Ã—288 pixels)
Classes: Open/Closed eyes with perfect labeling
Advantage: Specifically designed for eye state classification with 99%+ accuracy potential

Secondary Dataset: YawDD (Yawning Detection Dataset)

Download: http://www.site.uottawa.ca/~shervin/data/
Content: 322 videos, 29 subjects
Classes: Normal, Yawning, Talking
Use: Yawn detection enhancement

Tertiary Dataset: DROZY Database

Download: https://sites.google.com/view/drozydatabase/
Content: Real drowsiness scenarios
Subjects: 14 participants in actual drowsy states
